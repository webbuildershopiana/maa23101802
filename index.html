BUILDING TOMOROW'S NATION TODAY!
Maa Karni Infratech Private Limited (MKIPL) is a leading civil engineering company founded in 1991. The expert team of professionals at MKIPL are backed by several years of experience and exposure in road and civil constructions. Led by Mr. Rohit Agrawal, MKIPL assures prompt and efficient completion of work executed to the right specifications with the right qualified personnel, equipment and machinery. The past few years have seen MKIPL grow with steady progress in bid capacity and pre-qualification capability. 
-
Specialists in diverse civil projects for efficient infrastructure development.
-
Proven expertise in road construction, bridges, flyovers, and building works.
-
Leaders in executing projects with a focus on quality and efficiency.
