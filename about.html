HOW WE BECAME BEST AMONG OTHERS?
We would like to Introduce M/s. Maa Karni Infratech Pvt Ltd as a Civil Engineering Company involved in Execution of Roads (NH & SH), ROB, RUB, Flyovers, River Bridges and Buildings works in Various Types of Terrain in States of Rajasthan & other States.
We are a Team of Professionals with several years of combined Experience and Exposure in Road Macadamization and various Civil Constructions. We assure Prompt and Efficient Completion of Work, executed to the specifications by our Qualified Personnel, Machinery & Equipments. The Company has a large fleet of sophisticated equipment including Batch Type Hot Mix Plants, Boom Pumps, DGs, Soil Compactors, Tandem Rollers, Static Road Rollers, Back Hoe Loaders, Wheel Loaders, Motor grader, Pock lane, Excavators, Dozers, Tippers Big Size Concrete Batch Mix plants, Transit Mixers, Concrete Pavers, Concrete pumps, Stone Crushers, Hydra and Tower crane etc.
M/S Maa Karni Infratech Pvt Ltd (MKIPL) Company was formed in the year 1991 and reinstated in 2010 for Construction activity in PWD, RSRDCC, MORTH, NHAI, & RIICO Dept with Main Objective of Constructing Various Types of Infrastructure Developments like all types of Buildings, Bridges, Flyovers, Underpasses, Roads, Mechanized Earth Work, RCC Walls, Ducts, Drains and Compound Wall of all types of Terrain.
In the Projects of NH & SH Roads our aim is to achieve the target saving both time and economic resources without compromising on the work quality. MKIPL has witnessed a continuous growth in bid capacity and pre qualification capability.
Our Company is led by Shri Rohit Agarwal (An Engineer from Prestigious BITS Pilani) along with Smt. Kashish Bansal (CS and an MBA). Also, Our Company is with a vision of Think Tank and the Technical Advisor Mr. G.P. Sharma, former Chief Engineer, and Addl. Secretary.
