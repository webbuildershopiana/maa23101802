WHAT SETS US APART?
M/s. Maa Karni Infratech Pvt Ltd is a civil engineering company involved in execution of roads (NH & SH), ROB, RUB, flyovers, river, bridges and buildings works in various types of terrain pan India and majorly in the states of Rajasthan.
We are a team of professionals with several years of combined experience and exposure in road macadamization and various civil constructions. We assure prompt and efficient completion of work, executed to the right clients’ specifications by our qualified personnel, machinery & equipment. We have a large fleet of sophisticated equipment including hot mix plants, boom pumps, DGs, soil compactors, tandem and static road rollers, backhoe and wheel loaders, motor grader, pock lane, excavators, dozers, concrete batch mix plants, transit mixers, pavers, concrete pumps, stone crushers, hydra and tower cranes, etc.
MKIPL company was formed in 1991 and reinstated in 2010 for construction activity in PWD, RSRDCC, MORTH, NHAI, & RIICO Dept with the main objective of construction of various types of infrastructural development including but not limited to roads, buildings, bridges, flyovers, underpasses, roads, mechanized earth work, RCC walls, ducts, drains and compound walls in all types of terrain.
In NH & SH road projects, we aim to achieve the target and save both time and economic resources without compromising on the quality of work. We have witnessed a continuous growth in bid capacity and pre-qualification capability.
The company is registered as per rules with GST, ESI, PF, MSME and other company acts and compliances. Our banking partners include Kotak Mahindra, ICICI, Federal, HDFC, etc.
